## üêõ TROUBLESHOOTING

### Common Issues

**Issue:** AI agents not responding
**Solution:** Verify `ANTHROPIC_API_KEY` or `OPENAI_API_KEY` is set and valid.

**Solution:** Check Stripe/PayPal keys are correct and account is activated.
